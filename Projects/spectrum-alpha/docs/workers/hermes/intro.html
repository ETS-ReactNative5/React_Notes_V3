Hermes

Hermes (/ˈhɜːrmiːz/) is the messenger god, moving between the worlds of the mortal and the divine.

Hermes is our email worker. Hermes reads off of our Redis queue to process email content and deliver emails via SendGrid. Hermes is responsible for formatting content before it enters the email templates. All inbound jobs should contain all the required data for the final email.
